Glute Bridges

Instructions

Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat. Squeeze your glutes and lift hips until body forms straight line. Hold for 2 seconds at the top, then lower slowly.

Reps & Sets

15 reps, 3 sets

Focus Areas

Glutes, hamstrings, core

Form Tips

  • Keep your core engaged throughout
  • Squeeze glutes at the top of the movement
  • Don't arch your back excessively
  • Press through your heels, not your toes
